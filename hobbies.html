Anime

My Top 10 Anime

  1. Code Geass

    Code Geass has the best ending of all time. Some fans don't like the second half as a lot of things are happening all at once but I like that aspect. This series is very political and about using humans piloting robots to win the war between two nations.

  2. Attack On Titan

    Although I didn't watch it when it came out, this anime got so much hype in 2013. It onces again blew up when season 4 started. I think the story is unmatched as the plot reveals itself gradually after each episode. Opening 3 is my favorite as at first it wasn't as eye catching as opening 1 but slowly got addicting.

  3. Steins;Gate

    Steins;Gate is that anime that is on top of MyAnimeList but many watched it. Although slow at first, the mystery slowly unfolds as the story goes on because this is an anime about time travel. I was mindblown at the end, and this show is a very good mystery anime.

  4. Fate/Zero

    5. Before Demon Slayer, the studio Ufotable animated the Fate series. This came out in 2011 but the animation still holds up tremendously in animation. Part 2 is much better than part 1 as everything finally happens, and Fate/Zero is better than it's sequel Fate/Stay Night. I heard the 3 Fate movies are top tier too.

  5. One Piece

    I usually don't consider long running shounen shows to be a top 10 caliber anime but One Piece is the exception. Defintely the early parts are rough but once you get past that, you'll be hooked for life since the world building is number 1, the manga is superior, the recent animation is top tier, and it's a long series with memorable moments.

  6. Grand Blue

    7. Because I didn't watch Gintama, Grand Blue is the funniest anime I ever watched. It is literally about drinking and partying in college than it's "scuba diving" focus. There's a lot of misunderstandings and the manga is very good too.

  7. Demon Slayer

    I understand why people say it's carried by the animation because the animation is just that good but I still give props to the story. This show captures Japanese culture and gets better as the series goes on. The recent season is why I put it in front of Jujustsu Kaisen as both are very good.

  8. Haikyuu

    Haikyuu is the best sports anime because it gives attention to each character and doesn't make the action scenes over the top. Although it's predictable that the main team will always win, the series make you want to root for them and be invested in each match. My favorite player is Asashi. I didn't mind the animation change after season 3 but season 3 is still the serie's peak.

  9. Kaguya-sama: Love is War?

    10. I'm suprised the series gets better each time and it's usually the same comedic feel. Love is confusing and can be like a game so I love the unique spin this funny anime has about who will confess first.

  10. Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

    FMAB is considered the best anime of all time on the web but it's isn't that high for me. Every opening is good, the ending is up there, and it's consistently solid throughout with a mix of action, comedy, and drama in a military setting.
