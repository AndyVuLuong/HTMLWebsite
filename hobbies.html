Anime

My Top 10 Anime

  1. Code Geass 10/10 Best: Anime

    Code Geass has the best ending of all time. Some fans don't like the second half as a lot of things are happening all at once but I like that aspect. This series is very political and about using humans piloting robots to win the war between two nations.

  2. Attack On Titan 10/10 Best: Action

    Although I didn't watch it when it came out, this anime got so much hype in 2013. It onces again blew up when season 4 started. I think the story is unmatched as the plot reveals itself gradually after each episode. Opening 3 is my favorite as at first it wasn't as eye catching as opening 1 but slowly got addicting.

  3. Steins;Gate 10/10 Best: Story

    Steins;Gate is that anime that is on top of MyAnimeList but many watched it. Although slow at first, the mystery slowly unfolds as the story goes on because this is an anime about time travel. I was mindblown at the end, and this show is a very good mystery anime.

  4. Fate/Zero 9.5/10 Best: Animation

    Before Demon Slayer, the studio Ufotable animated the Fate series. This came out in 2011 but the animation still holds up tremendously in animation. Part 2 is much better than part 1 as everything finally happens, and Fate/Zero is better than it's sequel Fate/Stay Night. I heard the 3 Fate movies are top tier too.

  5. One Piece 9.5/10 Best: Long

    I usually don't consider long running shounen shows to be a top 10 caliber anime but One Piece is the exception. Defintely the early parts are rough but once you get past that, you'll be hooked for life since the world building is number 1, the manga is superior, the recent animation is top tier, and it's a long series with memorable moments.

  6. Grand Blue 9.5/10 Best: Comedy

    Because I didn't watch Gintama, Grand Blue is the funniest anime I ever watched. It is literally about drinking and partying in college than it's "scuba diving" focus. There's a lot of misunderstandings and the manga is very good too.

  7. Demon Slayer 9/10 Best: Animation2

    I understand why people say it's carried by the animation because the animation is just that good but I still give props to the story. This show captures Japanese culture and gets better as the series goes on. The recent season is why I put it in front of Jujustsu Kaisen as both are very good.

  8. Haikyuu 9/10 Best: Sports

    Haikyuu is the best sports anime because it gives attention to each character and doesn't make the action scenes over the top. Although it's predictable that the main team will always win, the series make you want to root for them and be invested in each match. My favorite player is Asashi. I didn't mind the animation change after season 3 but season 3 is still the serie's peak.

  9. Kaguya-sama: Love is War? 9/10 Best: Romance

    I'm suprised the series gets better each time and it's usually the same comedic feel. Love is confusing and can be like a game so I love the unique spin this funny anime has about who will confess first.

  10. Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood 9/10 Best: All Around

    FMAB is considered the best anime of all time on the web but it's isn't that high for me. Every opening is good, the ending is up there, and it's consistently solid throughout with a mix of action, comedy, and drama in a military setting.

My Top 10 Video Games
  1. Persona 5 Royal 10/10 Best: Game

    Persona 5 Royal is the greatest game of all time. It has the greatest soundtrack ever, an anime, aesthetic visuals, and the best turn based combat. It improves upon the original game by fixing the guns, adding more story and characters, and making exploration more fun.
  2. God of War Ragnarok 10/10 Best: Action

    This is the best game to play on PS5 with the best graphics, combat, and story. It improves upon the previous game by adding variety of bosses and locations, a deeper story, and more options to combat mainly characters and weapons. The skill tree is deep, there's a lot of customization, and I'm glad this is the final game to the franchise.
  3. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild Best: Adventure

    Nintendo took a risk with BoTW and it definitely paid off in this open world setting. What I love about the game is that you can climb almost everywhere within your stamia ad can fight the final boss anytime. I did play on Wii U, didn't mind the voice acting, and story is simple and straight forward.
  4. Half-Life Alyx Best: VR

    There will probably be no VR game that will be better than Valve's. You can see that the game felt complete in length and dedication since VR games are either short or based off of non VR ones. The gauntlet to pull any objects fall away, the gunplay, and the visual are all top tier here.
  5. Doom Eternal Best: Shooter

    Heavy metal and fast paced shooting is all you need to enjoy playing this game. Optimization is the best,.
  6. Resident Evil 2 Best: Survival/Horror

  7. Elden Ring

    This game is the accumulation of all the games FromSoftware has made. There's so much to discover in the open world and each boss fight is intense as ever. I played the Astrologer class with a mix between range spells and melee weapons.
  8. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Best: Fighting

    So many characters and mechanics have been added and optimized for the 4th installment of the franchise. Compared to traditional fighting games, it's very easy for anyone to play especially in a group setting. The added story mode, dlc, and multiplayer are all compelling additions to Smash Ultimate.
  9. Super Mario Odyssey Best:

    Although TLoZ BoTW is better, Super Mario Odyssey is up there and is the embodiment of fun. The art style is timeless, the cap brings new ways to transverse, and each world is distinct. It's pretty easy and combat can only reach a limit but playing it casually is satisfying enough.
  10. Red Dead Redemption 2 Best: Graphics

    This is the most realistic graphics ever and there's so much thought into it because it's RockStar Games. The story is very intersting an a cowboy setting and a plot about a group slowly falling apart. The game can be a bit too slow in everywthing but does add to the realism and the scope of the environment.
